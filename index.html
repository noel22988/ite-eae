The ITE Early Admissions Exercise (EAE) lets Secondary 4 and 5 students apply for 3-year Higher Nitec courses before results are released — based on your strengths, interests and portfolio. This tool helps you explore all 49 courses, choose up to 3, and craft your 100-word write-up for each.

1
Explore courses Chat with an AI advisor to find Higher Nitec courses that match what you enjoy and are good at.
2
Choose up to 3 courses Rank your choices in order of preference. Browse all 49 courses with entry requirements and cut-off references.
3
Write your application Answer 4 guiding questions in ~100 words per course. Get AI coaching questions and track your revisions.

Your work is saved automatically in your browser. Nothing is sent anywhere — everything stays on your device. When you're ready, use Print / Save PDF to keep a copy.

EAE applications are submitted at ite.edu.sg/apply-eae. This tool is for preparation only.
